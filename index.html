OUR STORY

Meet the Groom!
Nicholas Gregory Vitovitch takes joy in everyone and everything. His eccentricity and gregarious nature is a rarity and a gift to anyone with the patience for his antics. Underneath his colorful exterior, Nick is intensely intellectual with knack for the sciences and an insatiable curiosity for the Universe. His left-brained nature, however, doesn’t stop him from bursting into a duet at a moment’s notice. His sunny demeanor is infectious.

OUR STORY

Meet the Bride!
Read no further: Elizabeth Dorothy Gerger is amazing. She is a wellspring of creativity and takes no greater pleasure than inspiring those around her. Her distinctive energy combines the vigor of youth and the wisdom of an old soul. Though she lives in the hustle and bustle of New York City, Liz is never more in her element than when she spends an evening in the Pennsylvania woods.

OUR STORY

In their Junior year at The College of New Jersey, Nick and Liz starred in their spring musical, The Drowsy Chaperone: A comedic play within a play. In the show, Liz , the Chaperone, is swept off her drunken feet by Nick, the flamboyant and clumsy European, Aldofo. The play ends with their characters prophetically tying the knot on stage. They’ve been inseparable ever since...

OUR STORY

In the winter of their Senior year, Liz had the opportunity to study abroad in London. While Liz longed to share her adventures with Nick, Nick was drowning his sorrows in three bottles of Pinot Noir with Sandra & Peter Gerger across The Pond. The distance made them realize how much they needed each other. In a small London café, Liz received an internship offer from Nickelodeon in New York City!

OUR STORY

After graduation, Nick was hired as a Computer Scientist for SRI International in Princeton, NJ, while Liz freelanced for Nickelodeon! Nick would drive an hour to and from work, and Liz would make the 3-hour commute to and from Times Square. Finding time to see each other was becoming very difficult. That Christmas, they decided enough was enough, and leased their first apartment together in Princeton, NJ.

OUR STORY

Through thick and thin, sickness and health, achievement and loss, canceled trains, and all-nighters, Nick and Liz supported and celebrated each other.Not-so-subtle hints were dropped by both parties...

OUR STORY

On May 26, 2014, Nick took Liz on a picnic to Lake Galena, a favorite kayaking and hiking spot. Under a tree, in a sunny meadow filled with buttercups, Nick knelt down and asked Liz to share the rest of her life with him. She said yes!

OUR STORY

Nick & Liz are now settling into their second apartment in New York City, taking the next steps in their careers, overcoming setbacks, and persuing their dreams, all while planning their dream wedding, together! They would like to thank you for taking the time to visit their site and are so very excited to see you October 16, 2015!
